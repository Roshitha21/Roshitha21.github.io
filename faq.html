Frequently Asked Questions

For better help and understanding...!

+How do I book an appointment?

You can a) Call our Customer Care number: 9998887776 b) Or send an SMS/whatsapp to 9998887776 c) Or log on to www.healthylife.com and request an appointment d) Or directly walk in to the nearest Healthy life

+Is fasting required for my test?

Fasting for a test requires you to have no food or drink, except water, for 12 hours prior to sample collection. However not all tests require fasting.

+Are all the Doctors available round-the-clock?

No, the doctors are available as per pre-determined consultation hours. 

+What are the services available in Healthy Life?

Every Healthy Life offer Consultations for infants, children, adolescents, women, men and senior citizens. We also offer Lab Tests, Health Checks, Vaccinations, Dental services and Diabetes care. There is an Healthy Life Family Doctor at every centre and a treatment room that caters to medical requirements which includes minor procedures

+ Do you have the facility of sample pickup for diagnostics?

Yes. When you book our Health@Home service, a trained phlebotomist visits your home or workplace, as per your choice and collects samples.

+What are the working hours? Are you open on Sundays?

To make it convenient for you, your neighbourhood Apollo Clinic is open from 8 AM to 8 PM. We work on Sundays also, for more detail please call 9998887776.

+Can emergencies be treated at Healthy Life?

We are a primary health care provider and cater to everyday healthcare / medical issues. We have a treatment room where we attend to minor cuts, bruises, sores etc. Our experienced doctors can also stabilise a patient before referring him / her to the nearest hospital.

+Do you organise camps?

Yes, we do organise camps that focus on a single health issue. These camps are held both at Healthy Life hospitals and at large apartment complexes to make it easy for people from the neighbourhood to attend.
