ABOUT US

Healthy Life is a 150 bedded super speciality center built around the group’s 30 years experience, unparalleled consultant expertise and state-of-the-art technology. With multiple Centers of Excellence, chief among them – Institutes of Orthopedics, Institutes of Neurosciences, Institutes of Spine, Institutes of Pulmonology, and Critical Care, We are perhaps the most advanced medical center in south India. We are a fully equipped centre with a modern Emergency Room, state – of -the – art operating theatres, and ICUs with round the clock monitoring. We offer Speciality Clinic services in Pain Management, Bone Health, Uro-Nephro care, General Surgery and General Medicine. Our Emergency Rooms specialise in handling Cardiac emergencies, Stroke, Road accidents & trauma. This centrally located hospital has 37 ICUs equipped with ventilators (including noninvasive machines), BIPAP5, 6 super luxury executive suites, 12 private rooms, 28 semi private rooms, 50 multiple occupancy rooms (general wards). Our 10 emergency rooms, 25 out-patient consultation suites and 2 treatment rooms complete the infrastructure of the hospital.

MISSION

Our mission is to bring healthcare of International standards within the reach of every individual. We are committed to the achievement and maintenance of excellence in education, research and healthcare for the benefit of humanity

VISION

Healthy Life for the next phase of development is to ‘Touch a Billion Lives’. We are committed to the poorer sections of the society who need healthcare at an affordable cost. We serve our nation with full of dedication.

Team Work

What is important is that Healthy Life Hospitals achieved all this without compromising on the quality of services and nourishing the pro-people character that is closer to the hearts of its protagonists.
